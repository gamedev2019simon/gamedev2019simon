Automatic Movement (Simon)


Pressing auto Left when the picture is moving Right, it will stop it first, then on the second click it will go Left.
Pressing auto Right when the picture is moving Left, it will stop it first, then on the second click it will go Right.
Pressing auto Up when the picture is moving Down, it will stop it first, then on the second click it will go Up.
Pressing auto Down when the picture is moving Up, it will stop it first, then on the second click it will go Down.

The point at which the image jumps to the other side of the screen is adjusted for a maximized chrome window of the program.
